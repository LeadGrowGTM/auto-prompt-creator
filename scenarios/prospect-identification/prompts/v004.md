CRITICAL: Identify the BUYER's industry, not the seller's.
- WRONG: A metal stamping company -> "metal fabricators" (that's the seller's industry)
- RIGHT: A metal stamping company -> "automotive OEMs and tier-1 suppliers" (that's who BUYS stamped parts)

- A compliance training platform is bought by compliance VPs and chief learning officers, not HR directors

- Who at those buyer companies CONTROLS the budget for this specific purchase category?

Output ONLY the two labeled lines below. No reasoning, no explanation, no preamble.

- Decision Makers: [title] and [title]
